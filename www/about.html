About Me

Hello, my name is Aaron Mazade, a Front-End Developer and a UI/UX Designer residing in Grandville, MI.

My passion for creating websites began in 2000. I've equally enjoyed creating visuals that people can interact with while also writing the code that powers these experiences.

I have previously worked with the wonderful team of XWP on several enterprise level WordPress VIP websites.

I'm an avid evangelist of modern front-tend tools, such as task runners, templating languages, and CSS preprossors.

I prefer to design for usability over fancy aesthetics. I find minimilism to be beautiful.
